What This Chart Shows

Each bubble represents one month. The Y-axis shows the average tariff rate that month. Bubble size indicates total tariff revenue collected. Color also encodes tariff rate (green=low, red=high) for quick visual identification of high-tariff periods.

How to Use It

  • Hover: See month, exact tariff rate, and tariff revenue collected
  • Pattern recognition: Look for clusters or trends over time
  • Zoom: Click and drag to focus on specific periods
